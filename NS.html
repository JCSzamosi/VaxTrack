Vax Track Nova Scotia

Tracking how quickly Nova Scotia is moving its vaccine supply into human beings. All data are taken from covid19tracker.ca. This analysis is at the dose level and does not distinguish between first and second doses, or vaccine types.

Some provinces have dates where they appear to have administered more doses than were distributed to them. These doses are not included in wait-time counts.
