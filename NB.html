Vax Track New Brunswick

Tracking how quickly New Brunswick is moving its vaccine supply into human beings. All data are taken from covid19tracker.ca. This analysis is at the dose level and does not distinguish between first and second doses, or vaccine types.

Some provinces have dates where they appear to have administered more doses than were distributed to them. These doses are not included in wait-time counts.

What if we didn't wait?

In a counterfactual where we administer doses as soon as they come in, expanding capacity as necessary to ensure no doses wait more than 2 days:

code

vax_adm = what_if(dat_vax)

maxv = max(vax_adm$Doses, na.rm = TRUE)
vax_adm$rate_7 = roll_avg(vax_adm$Doses, 7)
vax_adm$rate_all = roll_avg(vax_adm$Doses, 'all')
tot = sum(vax_adm$Doses)
avg7 = vax_adm[nrow(vax_adm),'rate_7']
cum7 = vax_adm[nrow(vax_adm), 'rate_all']
msg0 = paste(format(tot, nsmall = 0, big.mark = ','), 'people would have been',
             'vaccinated so far.')
